How to Use BetweenLines

1. Export Your WhatsApp Chat

  1. Open WhatsApp on your phone.
  2. Go to the chat you want to analyze.
  3. Tap the three dots (menu) > More > Export Chat.
  4. Choose "Without Media" to exclude photos/videos (recommended for faster processing).
  5. The chat will download as a ZIP file.
  6. Extract the ZIP file on your phone.
  7. You'll get a .txt file - that's what you upload here.

2. Upload and Analyze

  1. Click "Choose File" and select the .txt file.
  2. Upload the file.
  3. Select yourself and the other person from the dropdowns.
  4. Wait for AI analysis (may take a few seconds).
  5. View your personality roles and percentages.
  6. Export results as JSON or PDF if desired.

3. Understanding Results

Each role shows how often you or them exhibit that behavior. Higher percentage means more dominant in that area.

  • Conversation Starter: Initiates after silences.
  • Snubber: Delays or short responses.
  • Romantic One: Affectionate messages.
  • Trouble One: Sarcastic or teasing.
  • At Fault: Blames others.
  • Listener: Asks questions.
  • Joker: Uses humor.
